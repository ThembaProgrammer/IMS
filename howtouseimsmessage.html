How To Use Ims Message

Introduction

ims message is a for fun and motivation website aimed at reducing Cyber-bulling, low self-esteem, negative thinking and low self confidence, by providing a service where people will advice, comment & compliment each other to promote positive thinking, social needs and positive image about other people.

you are looking 4?

In a search bar you will find the above mention name, which ask you who you are looking for?. You have to type the name of the person you want to search in full or a first name or last. Then click search.

You Want To?

This is a question that ask you what you want to do. You have to choose from the following 3 names: advice, comment & compliment, to write about other people.

Advice, Comment & Compliment

You have to choose between the 3 names what you want to do. This will lead you to a form to complete and submit it.

Form

This is a form you have to write about someone. You can also add a picture of someone you are writing about.
